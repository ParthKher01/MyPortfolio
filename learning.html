Team Work

Identification of Lifelong Learning Skill: According to me, teamwork is working in a group to achieve the expected result or goal. It involves multiple things, such as communication and coordination
Self-Assessment: Although I like to work independently, I am also good at teamwork. I understand the benefits of working in a team. But I find sometimes it is a daunting task. My recent work experience at "Amazon Warehouse" added valuable improvements, I improved my self by working in a team under pressure and take help of team member when necessary.
Description of the Artifact:

Packaging

Shorting

Deliver

During this period, We were responsible for sorting and scanning packages and ensuring that they reached the correct destinations. The work was fast-paced, and success depended on seamless coordination among team members. We had to communicate efficiently, assist each other with heavy lifting, and maintain a workflow to fulfill daily targets.
Rationale: "In conclusion, teamwork is an essential skill in both professional and academic settings. My experience at Amazon Warehouse reinforced the importance of collaboration in achieving shared goals, and I continue to apply these skills in my business management course, where group work is a key aspect of learning. Additionally, in my workplace, working effectively with others is crucial for success. Developing strong teamwork skills not only enhances productivity but also fosters better communication and problem-solving abilities, making it an invaluable asset in any career."
